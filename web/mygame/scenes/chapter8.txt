Shadows move across the walls as the lantern sways, its flame stubborn in the cool, damp air.

[i]“They’ve set the trial.”[/i]

Your throat tightens.

[i]“Trial?”[/i]

Alin nods once, jaw working. “Tomas. They’re calling it judgment under [i]the Law of Bone and Blood[/i].” 
Her gaze flickers toward you, as if weighing whether to go on. “[i]The Light[/i] will preside.”

“They say he harbored one of the cursed. A traveler. Someone… [i]touched by Venora[/i].” Alin’s fingers knot in the blanket. 
“And they’ll make a spectacle of him for it.”

Somewhere in the back of your mind, a whisper you don’t want to name:
[i]If you go, you might not be the only one watching.[/i]
